Manager Essentials

Welcome to Manager Essentials

Manager Essentials aims to provide you with what you need to get a headstart as a manager at the NHSBSA.

Here you will find a range of resources and learning opportunities that will allow you to ease into your management role effectively. We have taken the most common aspects of policy, procedure and system that you will encounter alongside a management development programme that will support you in implementing those approaches and developing excellent teams and enduring relationships.

You will also have the opportunity to consider the management style that is unique to you and consistent with the values of the NHSBSA.

FAQs

ESR Learning Management Frequently Asked Questions

This section provides access to common questions that may arise when using the ESR Learning Management system.

I have completed some training and my Compliance has not updated, what can I do?

To ensure your training has been completed correctly, please check the following.

  1. Using the My Compliance portlet, check you are subscribed or renewing a certification. If not subscribe to the certification and allow 24 hours for your record to be updated.
  2. On your learner home page, under e-learning enrolments check the status of the enrolment. If this is showing as incomplete, click the play button and complete the training, ensuring you click on the house to save your progress.

If neither of these resolves the issue, please contact the Training Team.

How do I review all the training required for my job?

Expand the Compliance Portlet and click on View My Compliance button.

Subjects that are required will show as red, amber or green. Blue competencies are additional training that you have completed but are not currently required for your role.

Is mandatory training that is completed in another organisation recognised by NHSBSA?

Training completed in other NHS organisations is automatically transferred via ESR. For training that is not, please contact the Learning and Development team with evidence of the training you want recognised.

Can I complete training whilst I am an applicant?

You can use the My Compliance portlet to complete e-learning prior to your start date if you wish.

Face to face or virtual classes should not be booked until you have started in post.

What do I do if my certification subscription status is "expired"?

Contact the Learning and Development team.

What do I do if the play button in the my learner home page is greyed out?

To resolve this access the learning via the My Compliance portlet.

Recruitment and Selection

Audience:

Managers

Delivery Mode:

Blended

Description:

This course aims to build manager's confidence and understanding of the recruitment process and to ensure all candidates experience a high quality, fair, and efficient recruitment journey. To build awareness of the practical implications and steps needed to successfully recruit the right talent for the NHS Business Services Authority (NHSBSA).

Candidates should experience an efficient and timely recruitment process that leaves them with a positive experience and impression of NHSBSA.

By completing this course you will be given the tools, advice and opportunities to ask questions related to end-to-end recruitment processes, ensuring we have a consistent approach in alignment with best practice from vacancy to appointment.

Let's talk about race

Audience:

Managers

Delivery Mode:

eLearning + Team Session

Description:

This training session explores race through a combination of self-paced eLearning and a facilitated CESI team discussion. Completion of the eLearning module is required before attending the team session.

The team session will revisit key parts of the eLearning, encouraging reflection and shared learning. It is designed to foster open dialogue and deepen understanding of race-related topics in a safe and supportive environment.
