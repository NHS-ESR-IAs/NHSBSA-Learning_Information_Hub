External Training Recognition

Most training transfers via the IAT process. If any records appear missing, email LEA_LD@nhs.net with evidence for manual review.

Certification Expired

Renew your competency: Re-access the course as soon as possible. If progress resets, ask your manager to confirm completion via email.

If you're locked out or concerned, contact LEA_LD@nhs.net.

Incorrect Compliance Requirements

Email LEA_LD@nhs.net with the incorrect details so your matrix can be updated.
